Hello there, I’m Leah! A travel and sustainability blogger living in San Diego, CA with a goal to see the world, while living a low impact lifestyle. These may seem like contrasting goals, but it absolutely can be done and I want to show you how! I believe life is always a little more exciting when I have a trip to look forward to and never leave the house without my water bottle and a reusable bag.
