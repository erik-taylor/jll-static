About Me

Hello there, I’m Leah, a 20-something living in San Diego, CA with a goal of traveling as much as my job will allow. I believe life is always a little more exciting when I have a trip to look forward to, even if it is just a weekend stay in the next town over.

At a young age, my parents instilled in me a love of adventure and a respect curiosity for experiencing different cultures. At age 12, I got my first passport. By age 13 I had already traveled to 4 new countries. I started traveling on my own during college, taking my first solo airplane trip just before my 19th birthday to go snowboarding in Colorado. A few days after i turned 20 I embarked on a semester abroad in Budapest, Hungary.

Through travel I have learned more about the world than from any lesson in school, more about the human race than I ever thought possible and more about myself than I ever dreamed. I started this blog to tell my travel stories and hopefully, share some helpful tips along the way. I hope you enjoy.

Some fun facts about me:

  • I grew up in Cincinnati, OH
  • I believe there is not a single food in this world that can not be improved with the addition of cheese
  • I have a sweet kitty named Luna and she loves to go on walks
  • I have been vegetarian for 10+ years
  • I am a huuuuuge fan of both The Lord of the Rings and Harry Potter universes
