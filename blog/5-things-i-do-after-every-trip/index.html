5 Things I Do AFTER Every Trip

By Jetlag Leah • Saturday, 02 February, 2019

Now that I am home, I am likely exhausted, a little dirty, and I just want to crawl into bed (remember those clean sheets from the 5 things I do BEFORE every trip) and sleep for 3 days.

Now that I am home, I am likely exhausted, a little dirty, and I just want to crawl into bed...

  1. Unpack immediately- there are 2 kinds of travelers, those who keep their suitcase in the corner of their room and just pick things out of it as they need them, and those who unpack as soon as they get home. I am a proud “immediate unpacker”, so as soon as I get home, I open my suitcase and put everything away, including my suitcase.

  2. Sanitize my toothbrush- I have an electric toothbrush with a travel case and that keep it free from junk in my bag but it the bristles sit in the case all wet from the last time I brushed my teeth. I soak my toothbrush head in antibacterial mouthwash for about 15 as soon as I get home to get rid off all the bacteria that was likely thriving in that dark, damp environment. While the ADA has no concrete evidence that this is necessary, it gives me piece of mind.

  3. Take a shower- While my toothbrush is getting clean, I clean myself too and go wild with the loofa because I likely haven’t properly exfoliated since I was home last.

  4. Stretch- Before I craw into bed, I like to get a full body stretch after a long day to travel. If I don’t get home too late I will do a longer yoga session, maybe 45 minutes to an hour but if it is past my bedtime I will just do a few sun salutations.

  5. Go through my pictures – I upload all the pictures from my camera to my computer and go through them, reliving all the highlights from my trip before passing out. Do you have a routine for when you get home?
